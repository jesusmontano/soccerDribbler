ANGRY REFEREE


  • AVOID THE PLAYERS AND COLLECT RED CARDS TO SEND THEM OFF
  • AVOID DISRUPTING PLAY BY STAYING AWAY FROM THE BALL

  • COLLECTING THE MYSTERY BLOCK MAY SLOW OR SPEED UP THE OPPONENTS

[A] = JOG LEFT

[D] = JOG RIGHT

[S] = JOG DOWN

[W] = JOG UP

[SPACE] = STOP

[J] = SPRINT LEFT

[L] = SPRINT RIGHT

[K] = SPRINT DOWN

[I] = SPRINT UP

[SPACE] = STOP
